WHO WE ARE
Praan was founded with the goal of reverting the environment
to what it used to be several decades ago.
We’re a deep tech company using patent-pending technology to purify ambient
air in cities without the use of any filters. We use scientific first principles along with
cutting edge hardware and software to make clean air accessible to all. We dream
of a world with clear blue skies, no masks, and healthy air!
At Praan, we believe that any technology built to tackle climate change or public
health catastrophes should be a net positive. Hence we spend our time on
building filterless, adaptable, and affordable devices which solve the problem at
scale. Our technologies adapt to changing weather conditions and ensure that all
captured pollutants are recycled.
We’re a team of engineers, designers, computer scientists, biologists, business
minds, and dreamers, who are working towards making the future more positive
and exciting. Our team brings over 158 people from more than 70 cities around
the world to solve extremely challenging problems together.

Our team consists of some of the brightest minds in the world bringing the best
experiences from Tesla, SpaceX, Microsoft, Amazon, Intel, Philips, Facebook,
United Nations, etc.
